**Q: so what heapify function really do is creating the max heap to swap max numbers to the right ?**



Short correct answer



heapify does NOT create the whole max heap by itself.



heapify only fixes ONE part of the array so that the “max-heap rule” is satisfied at a given index.



What heapify REALLY does (exact definition)



**heapify ensures that the element at a given index is the largest among itself and its children, and if not, it moves the largest element upward.**



That’s it.



No more. No less.

Who does what (clear separation)

--------------------------------



1️⃣ heapify

Fixes violations



Does not care about sorted order



Does not move max to end



2️⃣ heapSort

Shrinks heap



Produces sorted array
